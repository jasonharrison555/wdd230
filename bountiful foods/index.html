Treasure Island Beach

Take a trip to the Treasure Island Beach. It's sandy, non-rocky bottom is perfect swimming. If you go to the more northern part of the beach where the clear water is, you'll find that this makes a great spot for snorkeling. More north and you will see some rocks. Going across their you can find a hidden cove! Come and visit and you won't be disappointed!
