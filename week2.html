Design Principles Document

Repition

Walmart

https://www.walmart.com/

Walmart's website uses a lot of repition. One of the things they do is have circles with images of products in them. Walmart also shows repition by using the same colors. They like to use the colors blue, white and yellow. Those colors appear in many places.

Alignment

Amazon

https://www.Amazon.com/

Amazon uses the principle of alignment quite a bit in their website. One of the things they do is horizontally align their buttons and navigation bars. They also like to align their pictures together. They also aligned the search and sign bars with the pictures that are below.

Contrast

Anytime Fitness

https://www.anytimefitness.com/

Anytime Fitness uses a lot of contrast in their website. If you look at their fonts, they aren't all the same. They'll use a bolder font and below it use a skinnier font. Another example of contrast that they use are the color they use. They used a darker purple for the background and made the font white. When the background was white they used purple for the font color.
